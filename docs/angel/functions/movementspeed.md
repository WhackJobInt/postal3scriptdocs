## MovementSpeed

<p>Adds extra speed to the movement speed of the entity. (NPCs only)</p>

<div class="admonition warning">
<p class="admonition-title">Postal III Ultrapatch+Angel v1.3.0+ Feature</p>
<p>The following function will only work in <b>Postal III Ultrapatch+Angel v1.3.0 or higher</b>.</p>
</div>

<h1>Syntax</h1>
`MovementSpeed 0.0` -- Normal speed.</p>
`MovementSpeed 15.0` -- Increased overall speed.</p>
`MovementSpeed -15.0` -- Decreased overall speed.</p>
